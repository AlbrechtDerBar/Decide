Instructions

This is the instructional page that has information on all of the different web pages that make up this site.

Flip A Coin

A simple coin flipper. it flips a coin and is either heads or tails.

Roll Dice

Roll a specified amount of some dice. select the die type and input an amount to roll and it will tell you the cumulative total of all the dice after the roll. for die types they are represented by d* where * is the number of sides on the die, so a d4 will roll between 1-4.

Random List

This selects 1 random item from the list of items you input.

8-Ball

Ask the 8-ball a question and it will give you an answer.

Stick Pick

Put names on the "sticks" and the computer will organize them by stick size and output the order from largest to smallest

Russian Roulette

A game of Russian Roulette. there is one bullet in the chamber of 6. To start load the gun to put the bullet in a random chamber. then either spin the chamber or fire the gun to see if you survive. you can play by either continuously firing the gun, and the game will last a maximum of 6 rounds, or you can spin the chamber each turn to make the game longer.

Cards

card functions for shuffling a deck and drawing a card.
